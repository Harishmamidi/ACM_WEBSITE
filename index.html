About SCSVMV-ACM

We are the Association for Computing Machinery(ACM), Student Chapter, Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswathi Viswa Mahavidyalaya, Enathur or in short, SCSVMV-ACM

Our Vision

ACM brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‐long learning, career development, and professional networking.

Our Works

Competitive Programming

We enhance the skills of the students to crack their technical rounds and interview rounds of top companies easily by making them practice in various sites like HackerRank,HackerEarth,GeeksforGeeks and InterviewBit and teach them various programming languages such as Python,Java and Kotlin.

Mobile Application Development

This ACMchapter aims at instilling interest for developing apps for platforms like iOS, Android, Windows as well as cross-platform app development. App developement involves designing UI libraries for apps and using them as well as developing backend APIs and data structures and implementing them.
